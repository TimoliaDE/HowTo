## General Questions

### What is the server IP again?
You can reach our Minecraft server using the address `play.timolia.de`. We wish you lots of fun on our network!

### Is there a Discord server?
You can join our Discord server via [https://timolia.de/discord](https://timolia.de/discord). Here you can exchange ideas with other users, get support, or participate in giveaways.
You can find all further information in our [Discord explanation](/discord/).

### What happened to 1.8?
#### Arcade
Arcade will be revised in the future and will be available again. We ask for your understanding that we cannot currently provide more precise information regarding a possible release date.

#### JumpWorld
The maps from the JumpWorld 1.8 server are still available in our database and can be played.

### Where did CityBuild go?
Everything started with CityBuild. On May 7th, 2011, Tim founded Timolia's CityBuild.
Unfortunately, City had to close on December 16th, 2018, for various reasons. You can find more information about this [here](http://timolia.de/city).

### What ranks are there?
- **<span class='administrator'>Administrator</span>**:
These team members form the management of Timolia. They handle administration, organization, and team leadership.

- **<span class='sysadmin'>SysAdmin</span>**:
System administrators plan, install, configure, and maintain Timolia's technical systems. They perform updates and ensure a smooth gaming experience every day.

- **<span class='developer-plus'>Developer+</span>**:
They have many years of experience as developers on Timolia and are available for internal development questions. They also handle Timolia plugins, develop new features and game modes, and fix potential bugs.

- **<span class='developer'>Developer</span>**:
Developers take care of Timolia plugins, develop new features and game modes, and look after the bugs—pardon, features—on Timolia.

- **<span class='moderator'>Moderator</span>**:
Moderators are long-standing and particularly experienced team members who possess unique expertise in their respective fields. They serve as internal contacts for questions and often coordinate individual task areas.

- **<span class='supporter-plus'>Supporter+</span>**:
They have comprehensive specialist knowledge and are available for all questions and reports. They are the first point of contact for players with concerns.

- **<span class='supporter'>Supporter</span>**:
Supporters are available for all basic questions and reports and are the first point of contact for players with concerns.

- **<span class='headbuilder'>HeadBuilder</span>**:
They lead and organize the building team and serve as the primary contact for all builders. They also handle the selection and training of new builders and act as the interface between the build team and general project organization.

- **<span class='builder-plus'>Builder+</span>**:
Builder+ members take on additional organizational responsibility due to their high level of experience in both level design and the team. For example, they lead projects or act as mentors for new builders.

- **<span class='builder'>Builder</span>**:
The building team creates new maps and lobbies for all occasions and game modes on Timolia. They manage the architectural design of new modes and handle external map submissions.

- **<span class='content-plus'>Content+</span>**:
They have extensive experience in the creative field. They take on leadership tasks, coordinate projects, and support the content team as points of contact.

- **<span class='content'>Content</span>**:
As the creative core, they are responsible for various needs ranging from graphic design to translation.

- **<span class='youtuber'>YouTuber</span>**:
YouTubers are people in the Minecraft scene who have become known through YouTube (or in individual cases, Twitch). For smaller YouTubers, there is the **<span class='mvp'>VIP</span>** rank. You can learn everything about these ranks [here](/ranks/youtube/).

- **<span class='expert'>Premium Ranks</span>**:
Players with one of our permanent premium ranks receive certain advantages. You can find out which rank offers which benefits [here](/ranks/premium/).

**Everything regarding applications can be found [here](/team/apply/).** If you have problems with your premium rank, you can find help [here](/ranks/premium/).

We are also happy to support you on our [Discord](https://timolia.de/discord), where you can communicate your concern at any time in the `#support` channel.

### How does the kick system work?
We have a quite complex and unique kick system to provide both premium and normal players with a great gaming experience without long waiting times.
Every player receives an individual "kick value" (KV) when joining the network, based on their ingame rank. The higher the rank, the higher the "kick value". A player with a higher "kick value" kicks the player with the lowest "kick value" if a server is full.

| Rank                                    | Kick Value |
|-----------------------------------------|------------|
| User                                    | 0          |
| <span class='pro'>Pro</span>            | 300        |
| <span class='pro'>Pro+</span>           | 400        |
| <span class='pro'>Subscriber</span>     | 400        |
| <span class='mvp'>MVP</span>            | 500        |
| <span class='mvp'>MVP+</span>           | 600        |
| <span class='mvp'>Subscriber+</span>    | 600        |
| <span class='expert'>Expert</span>      | 800        |
| <span class='expert'>Subscriber*</span> | 900        |
| <span class='youtuber'>Youtuber</span>  | 900        |

If, for example, a person with the Expert rank enters a full server, a random other person with the lowest KV is sent back to one of the games lobbies. The kicked person's kick value then increases by 1. On the next attempt, it is therefore more likely that the person will not be kicked, as others now have a lower kick value. As soon as a game starts, everyone is assigned their default kick value again.

### Where can I appeal a ban/mute?
You can appeal a ban or mute at any time. You can submit your appeal in a support ticket on our [Discord](https://timolia.de/discord).

:::note
The more often you have been banned or muted within the last year, the less likely it is that your unban request will be successful.
:::

## Games

### Where is teaming allowed?
Teaming is explicitly forbidden in 4rena FFA and InTime. In 4rena 2v2, crossteaming is forbidden, and in BrainBow, teaming is only tolerated between two teams. Teaming in the Timolian Games carries no consequences. Further information can be found in the official [Rulebook](/rules/games/).

### Where can I submit maps?
In our Discord forum channel [#ideas](https://discord.com/channels/407554118887014402/1047084675627221092), built arenas can be presented and will be reviewed by all team members, especially our building team.

### Am I allowed to use arenas from Timolia?
We do not offer any maps found on our network for download, as it is not appreciated when our arenas are distributed without prior consent. However, we will not take action against the use of our maps; we simply ask that you credit the builders if you should use a Timolia map.

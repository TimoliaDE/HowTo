As the Timolia team, we offer you as a content creator the opportunity to apply for two different ranks. These provide access to exclusive premium features and useful ingame tools.

## What are the requirements for these ranks?
- From a subscriber count of ~2000 on YouTube, you can apply for the VIP rank on Timolia. Streamers also have the possibility to apply for this rank from an average viewership of ~30.
- From ~6000 subscribers on YouTube, you can apply for the YouTuber rank. Streamers also have the possibility to apply for this rank from an average viewership of ~50 viewers.
- For both ranks, it is important that videos/streams about Timolia are already present on your channel. These should be produced with high quality and meet the standards of our server. A high level of recognition is also advantageous and can play a significant role in the selection process.

## What advantages do the ranks offer me?

### VIP (MVP+)
- The VIP rank basically offers all features of the [MVP+ rank](premium.md).
- Access to the Nick tool is provided via the item in the hotbar or the command `/nick`.
- Players with the VIP rank can use the `/forcemap` command in games with a voting system.
- VIPs can use `/start` to start a game.

### YouTuber Rank
- The YouTuber rank offers the same features as the [Expert rank](premium.md).
- Access to the Nick tool is provided via the item in the hotbar or the command `/nick`.
- YouTubers receive a kick-value of 900. (You can read about how the kick system works [<strong>here</strong>](/faq/#wie-funktioniert-das-kick-system).)
- In the lobby, YouTubers can knock players away by left-clicking.
- The nametag is marked in <span class='youtuber'>**purple**</span>.
- A private JumpWorld and 1vs1 lobby is provided for YouTubers.
- YouTubers can use the `/forcemap` command in games with a voting system.
- YouTubers can use `/start` to start a game.
- In the Games lobby, YouTubers can make themselves invisible with `/vanish`.
- With `/giftpremium <Name>`, YouTubers can give away 3 days of Pro, 3 times a day.

More great features for YouTubers and VIPs will follow in the future.

## How do I apply for a rank?
If you believe that you meet all the criteria and would like to apply for a rank on Timolia, you have 3 ways to apply for the rank.

- By applying on our [application page](https://apply.timolia.de).
- Via a ticket on our [Discord](https://timolia.de/discord) in the `#support` channel.

In any case, we decide individually based on various criteria whether one of the ranks is suitable for you.

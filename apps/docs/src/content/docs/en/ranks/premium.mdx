## FAQ & Support

### My rank is not there, what can I do?
Payments can take time. If your rank hasn't arrived after **24 hours**, contact us on Discord or via email with your purchase details.

### Do I keep my rank after a name change?
**Yes.** Ranks are bound to your unique UUID, not just your name. Your rank will stay with you after a rename.

### Can I transfer my rank?
**No.** Ranks are account-bound. If you made a mistake during the order (e.g., wrong username), please contact support immediately.

### What happens after my rank expires?
Premium features become **passive**. You won't lose your progress (e.g., placed JumpWorld entities or saved 1vs1 kits remain), but you cannot actively use them or create new ones until you renew your rank.

### Will my rank be refunded?
As a rule, payments for ranks are **non-refundable**.

## §1 - General
In general, the [LabyMod VoiceChat](https://www.labymod.net/en/voicechat/rules) and other Voice Chat mods are subject to the [general rules](/rules/games) of the Minecraft network.

## §2 – Excessive/Disruptive Noises
For example, noises used with the intent to annoy others. This includes screaming, music and sound bots, voice changers, etc.

## §3 - Provocation
Such as disrespectful and provocative behavior towards other users of the Voice Chat, etc.

## §4 - Discrimination
For example, insulting individuals who identify with a different sexual orientation, gender, origin, or religion, as well as people with disabilities, or based on their appearance, etc.

## §5 - Racism
Such as insulting individuals based on their ethnicity, skin color, appearance, etc.

## §6 - Extremism
For example, the dissemination of radical phrases, etc.

## §7 - Advertising
Such as excessive, disruptive, or repetitive advertising, etc.

## §8 - Harassment/Threats
For example, consistent teasing, inappropriate sexual remarks, threats of violence, etc.

## §9 - Bypassing a Mute
Such as connecting to the Voice Chat with other accounts to bypass a mute, etc.

## §10 - Trading
For example, attempting to sell or begging for cosmetics, emotes, or other items from other users, etc.

:::note
The team reserves the right to sanction certain actions (even outside of the Minecraft and TeamSpeak servers) that are not explicitly listed in the rules.
:::

---
title: Freebuild
description: All rules defining Freebuild can be found here.
---

## §1 - Built Structures
- All structures are subject to Timolia's general rules.
- The same applies to text on signs or in books.
- Structures whose sole purpose is to disturb other players are prohibited.

## §2 - Griefing
- Structures that are not built within a claimed area may generally be modified as desired.
- Exploiting bugs to bypass building restrictions is prohibited.
- Building unsightly structures with no apparent purpose directly next to another player's plot is prohibited.

## §3 - Plots
- Claiming plots with the intention of disturbing another player is prohibited.
- Plots must be used actively.
- Plots from inactive players that do not contain significant structures will be removed irregularly.
- The team reserves the right to remove plots if they negatively affect the gameplay of other players.

## §4 - Interactions with other Players
- Setting traps for other players is prohibited.
- Intentionally causing damage to other players is prohibited.
- Scaming other players for items or coins is prohibited.

## §5 - Bugs
- Exploiting any bugs for personal advantage is prohibited.
- Bugs affecting the game must be reported to the team. Reporting serious bugs may result in a reward for the reporter.

## §6 - Secondary Accounts/Alt Accounts
- Secondary/alt accounts may not be used to enlarge the main account's plot. 
- Secondary/alt accounts may not be used to purify items at the Alchemist for the main account.

:::note
The team reserves the right to sanction certain actions (even outside of the Minecraft and Discord servers) that are not explicitly listed in the rules.
:::

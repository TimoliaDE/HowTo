## Game Concept
Are you good at Jump and Runs and want to prove your skills? Then DNA is just the thing for you! The goal is to be the first player to reach the upper limit of 32 blocks.

<YouTube id="https://youtu.be/0xjnQXqb7-A" />

<p></p>

## How it works
- Vote for the map of your choice with `/vote <Map>` or by clicking in the chat.
- At the start of the game, everyone is assigned a color, which represents the color of your Jump and Run.
- After a few seconds, the first blocks spawn; every time you climb a block, another one is spawned and the previous one is removed.
- Right-click on a block to set a checkpoint.
- The more checkpoints you set, the longer it takes for the next block to appear.
- Blocks that change color are Special Blocks. These can provide special items or effects.

---

## Special Items and Effects

- **Snowball**: Shoot your opponents down.
- **Rotten Egg**: Cause nausea for your opponent.
- **Speed**: Jump faster and further with the Speed effect.
- **JumpBoost**: Jump faster and further with the Jump Boost effect.

## Premium Features

- Premium players have up to four votes in the voting system, depending on their rank.
- A favorite map can be added to the map voting at any time.
- Premium players receive up to three times more Pixels.
- Be displayed as a normal player with `/togglerank`.

You can read about which Premium rank offers which advantages [here](/ranks/premium/).

## Game Concept

In BrainBow, you can prove your archery skills together with your team. The team with the last survivors wins the round and secures the victory.

<YouTube id="https://youtu.be/EPi7lAyV3R8" />

<p></p>

## How it works
- Vote for the map of your choice with `/vote <Map>` or by clicking in the chat.
- Up to four teams fight against each other in the arena.
- At the start of the game, all teams are teleported to their bases, from which the game begins.
- There are different blocks on the ground. If you stand on one of the special blocks, you can use its ability.
- Redstone blocks shoot TNT, which must be fired into the opponent teams' score fields.
- A hit by an opponent team subtracts one heart from your team; a hit by your team adds half a heart.
- Once a team has no hearts left, its players can no longer respawn. When the last person is killed, the team is eliminated.
- The last team alive wins the battle and receives the most points.
- With 1000 points earned, the BrainBow bow can be unlocked in the lobby.

## The Blocks

| Block | Name | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| <span style="color:#CF0101">Redstone</span> | TNT Block | Must be shot into the opponent's score fields. |
| <strong>Coal</strong> | Bomb | Kill opponents, destroy TNT or TNT blocks, and boost yourself upwards. |
| <span style="color:#0000FF">Lapis Lazuli</span> | Ender Pearl | Helps with fast movement across the map. |
| <span style="color:#64FE2E">Emerald</span> | Poison Vine | Poisons players within a certain radius. The effect only wears off back in your base. |
| <span style="color:#D8D8D8">Quartz</span> | Snow Field | "Freezes" players within a certain radius. |

## Premium Features
- Premium players have up to four votes in the voting system, depending on their rank.
- A favorite map can be added to the map voting at any time.
- Premium players receive up to three times more Pixels.
- Premium players can kick other people from a full team during the voting phase.
- Be displayed in the normal standard color with `/togglerank`.

You can read about which Premium rank offers which advantages [here](/ranks/premium/).

## Game Concept
In Suspicious, there are two teams: Crewmates and Imposters. The task of the Crewmates is to complete all their tasks while avoiding being killed by the Imposters.

<p></p>

## How it works // Crewmate
- Try to complete all your tasks one after another. The map will help you find the right locations.
- As a Crewmate, you don't know who the Imposter is. Try to identify the person acting **suspicious**!
- During meetings, you have the opportunity to vote for the person you believe is an Imposter to eliminate the threat.
- The Crewmates win the game if they complete all tasks or vote out all Imposters.

---

## How it works // Imposter
- Use your killstick to eliminate all Crewmates. However, you must keep an eye on the cooldown!
- Try to divert suspicion onto others by pretending to complete tasks like the rest of the Crewmates.
- Other players cannot see which items you are holding in your hand.
- Use sabotages tactically to distract Crewmates or suddenly disappear into one of the vents to quickly jump between rooms.
- The Imposters win the game if they kill all Crewmates.

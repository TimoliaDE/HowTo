## Gameplay
Welcome to the Timolian Games! Here you can prove your PvP skills in battle against 23 other players. In an infinitely large world, you will find beacons everywhere that spit out items as you approach them, though they disappear again soon after.

<p></p>

## How it works
- All fighters spawn in an infinitely large, randomly generated world. However, this world has been slightly modified: new biomes and custom structures have been added.
- White beacons spawn in this world, turning blue as you approach and spitting out random items.
- If you stand on a beacon while it disappears, you will be teleported near an opponent. From then on, a particle trail will show you the direct path to them.
- Decide for yourself when you feel ready for battle. But be careful! Someone can teleport to you at any time. A warning sound will play when this happens.

---

## Special Items
Normal white beacons occasionally drop special items. When you kill a player, a red beacon appears, which drops one of 10 special items after a short time. The same happens with purple beacons, which can only be found in Nether or End biomes.

- **Shield**: This item spawns a glass shell around you.
- **Flames**: Use this to shoot flames at your opponents.
- **Jump**: The Jump item launches you a few blocks upward.
- **Grenade**: With the grenade, you can cause explosions near your enemies.
- **Rain**: If you are on fire, you can extinguish yourself with this item.
- **Healing**: Everyone within the range of the hearts receives healing.
- **Gust of Wind**: A small gust of wind sends you and your opponents stumbling.
- **Dogs**: This spawns three dogs that will protect you from now on.
- **Wither**: With the Wither item, you can fire Wither explosions.
- **Thor's Hammer**: Thor's Hammer generates a dangerous bolt of lightning.

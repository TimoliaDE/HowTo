## Game Concept
Splun is a combination of **Spl**eef and Jump and R**un**, making it the perfect quick game for in-between.

<p></p>

## How it works
- Vote for the map of your choice with `/vote <Map>` or by clicking in the chat.
- Try to survive as long as possible by jumping from block to block.
- Everything you touch disappears beneath your feet.
- You can use various items to influence your opponents or move faster.

---

## Special Items
* **Crossbow**: Shoot other players with an arrow.
* **Blaze Rod**: Restores blocks beneath you.
* **Chorus Fruit**: Teleport to a random location on the map by eating the fruit.
* **Egg**: Throw eggs to spawn blocks.
* **Eye of Ender**: Grants you three double jumps.
* **Ender Pearl**: Receive an Ender Pearl.
* **Fireball**: Throw a fireball that explodes after a short time.
* **Golden Apple**: Grants the ability to fly.
* **Obsidian**: All opponents receive the Blindness effect.
* **Rocket**: Allows you to jump higher.
* **Redstone Torch**: Launches all players into the air.
* **Snowball**: Throw snowballs to destroy blocks.
* **Boots**: Allows you to run faster.
* **Sword**: Knock your opponents off the platform.
* **Soul Sand**: All opponents receive the Slowness effect.
* **Cobweb**: Place a spider web to hinder others from running.
* **Pickaxe**: Allows you to mine blocks.
* **Stone**: Creates a hovering platform beneath your feet.
* **TNT**: Blocks you touch turn into TNT.

## Premium Features
* Premium players have up to four votes in the voting system, depending on their rank.
* A favorite map can be added to the map voting at any time.
* Premium players receive up to three times more Pixels.
* Be displayed as a normal player with `/togglerank`.

You can read about which Premium rank offers which advantages [here](/ranks/premium/).

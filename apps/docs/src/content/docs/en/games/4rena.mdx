## Game Concept

4rena combines PvP with tactical gameplay elements. Prove yourself against three other players with limited items. Each round, choose which equipment is available to you, but keep in mind: items can only be used once!

<YouTube id="https://youtu.be/DTOIOy35JXc" />

<p></p>

## How it works
- Vote for the arena of your choice with `/vote <Map>` or by clicking in the chat.
- After the voting phase ends, all players are teleported to the arena.
- You have several chests with items at your disposal; however, you can only use these once.
- Press the button on the redstone lamp to signal the start of the fight.
- All players are teleported into the arena; whoever is the last one standing wins the round.
- If a person wins a total of five rounds, they have won the game.

---

## Premium Features
- Premium players have up to four votes in the voting system, depending on their rank.
- Premium players receive up to three times more Pixels.
- Be displayed in the normal standard color with `/togglerank`.

You can read about which Premium rank offers which advantages [here](/ranks/premium/).

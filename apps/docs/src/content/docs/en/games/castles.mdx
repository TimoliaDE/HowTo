## Gameplay
Castles is a tactical game where you must capture the flag of the opposing teams, either alone or together with your teammates. Each team consists of a maximum of 4 players and starts the game in its own castle.

<YouTube id="https://youtu.be/ZeydLQxk_Hg" />

<p></p>

## How it works
- Vote for the map of your choice with `/vote <Map>` or by clicking in the chat.
- The goal of the game is to steal the flag from the enemy castle, bring it back to your own base, and then kill all enemy players.
- With the gold found in the Enderchest at your base and in chests on other islands, you can build structures or buy items.
- In the various shops, you will find many useful items that allow you to attack the enemy base tactically or as quickly as possible.
- As long as your team possesses a flag in its own base, you can respawn as often as you like. 
- If all flags have been captured by opposing teams, they can no longer respawn unless a teammate can win a flag back.
- If the game lasts a long time because a team has barricaded itself, the winner can be determined through "Flag Power"—owning the majority of flags over a longer period. 
- The game ends as soon as all enemy players have died permanently.

## Point Distribution in Castles
- If you leave the round before your team is eliminated, you receive no points.
- Kills are doubled at the end (**Kills * 2**).
- Flag captures are multiplied by eight at the end (**Captures * 8**).
- If you are on the winning team, the points are doubled at the end (**x2**).
- If you have the most kills, you receive **+15 points**.
- If you have the second-most kills, you receive **+10 points**.
- If you have the third-most kills, you receive **+5 points**.
- If you invested more than 60% of the collected gold in structures, you receive **+12 points**.
- Finally, this formula is applied: 

$$
3,5 \cdot \sqrt{\frac{7 \cdot \text{Total Points}}{3}}
$$

## Premium Features
- Premium players have up to four votes in the voting system, depending on their rank.
- A favorite map can be added to the map voting at any time.
- Premium players receive up to three times more Pixels.
- Premium players can kick other people from a full team during the voting phase.
- Be displayed in the normal standard color with `/togglerank`.

You can read about which Premium rank offers which advantages [here](/ranks/premium/).
